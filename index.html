Our Legacy

Established in 1980 under the supervision and guidelines of the charitable organization "Tha'leemudheen Trust", Rahmaniya has grown into a beacon of education in Salamath Nagar, Meppadam.


Currently, the institution has become a Higher Secondary School recognized by the government of Kerala, serving students from LKG to XII with a commitment to academic proficiency and moral nurturing.
